IRINS (Indian Research Information Network System)


IRINS is web-based Research Information Management (RIM) service developed by the Information and Library Network (INFLIBNET) Centre.


The portal facilitates the academic, R&D organisations and faculty members, scientists to collect, curate and showcase the scholarly communication activities and provide an opportunity to create the scholarly network.


IRINS provides more significant exposure for their research contributions to the international community and brings funding opportunities from the national and international agencies


It helps the scholars to find mentors or advisors for their research in the respective domain and locate courses in their subject interests and research focus of the department and organization.


To establish your profile on IRINS portal please contact the library or send an email to office_library@iitj.ac.in with subject line - IRINS Profile.
